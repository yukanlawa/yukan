Mission 01 / 任務一

Ecology & Survival (生態與生存)

Smangus Giant Trees

Scenario / 情境故事

You are hiking in the giant tree forest in Smangus (司馬庫斯). Suddenly, it starts raining heavily! Your Atayal guide says, "Don't worry, nature provides umbrellas."
(你走在司馬庫斯的巨木林中，突然下起大雨！長老告訴你大自然就是最好的雨傘...)

Q: Which local plant do Atayal people traditionally use as a natural umbrella or shelter roof? (哪種尖石常見植物，最常被泰雅族人用來做天然雨傘或搭建臨時棚頂？)

💡 English Key Word: Shelter (庇護所 / 避雨處).
"We can use banana leaves to build a shelter."
Mission 02 / 任務二

Festival & Sharing Value (祭典與分享)

Smyus / Harvest Festival

Scenario / 情境故事

It’s harvest season in Jianshi! Every summer, the community celebrates the Millet Harvest Festival (Smyus). Your family made a lot of delicious bamboo rice (竹筒飯).
(尖石鄉小米豐收祭到了！全家人一起慶祝，製作了許多美味的竹筒飯...)

Q: According to the Atayal core value "Gaga", what is the best way to handle a big harvest? (根據泰雅族核心道德規範「Gaga」，面對豐收時最理想的做法是什麼？)

💡 English Key Word: Generously (慷慨地 / 大方地).
"Atayal people share their crops generously."
Mission 03 / 任務三

Art & Ancestral Identity (工藝與認同)

Atayal Weaving Art

Scenario / 情境故事

You visit a weaving studio in Jianshi. You see beautiful red, black, and white patterns on the clothes. The weaver points to the diamond shapes (菱形紋).
(你參觀尖石鄉工藝教室，看著紅白黑相間的美麗布料。織女指著衣服上的「菱形紋」...)

Q: What do the traditional diamond patterns on Atayal clothing represent? (泰雅族傳統服飾上隨處可見的「菱形紋（Bnkis）」象徵著什麼涵義？)

💡 English Key Word: Ancestors (祖先 / 祖靈).
"We must respect our ancestors and follow their wisdom."
